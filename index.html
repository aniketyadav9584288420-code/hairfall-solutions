Common Hair Problems

Heavy Hairfall

Hairfall can happen due to stress, hormonal imbalance, poor diet and weak hair roots.

Dandruff

Dandruff is usually caused by dry scalp, fungal infection or wrong hair products.

Thin Hair

Thin hair can result from protein deficiency, excessive styling and poor nutrition.

Genetic Hairfall

Genetic hairfall happens due to family history and gradual weakening of hair follicles.
